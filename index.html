Abstract

Digital Salon is an AI and physics-driven 3D hair grooming and simulation tool. It integrates breakthrough real-time strand-based hair simulator and copilot 3D generation systems to allow users to interactively generate hairstyles through text prompts, configure hair dynamics with various interactions and obtain the AI-rendered images.
